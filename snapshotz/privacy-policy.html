SnapShotz Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 23, 2025

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

SnapShotz (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use the SnapShotz mobile application (the “App”) and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Name: The name you use to start the game.
  • Game Data: Information such as room details, game state, and leaderboard scores.
  • Photos: Photos uploaded or selected for a game are used to enable gameplay and may be stored temporarily. We only use photos for gameplay, support, safety, and technical operation of the App.
  • Photo Metadata: Metadata associated with photos, such as timestamps, location, or file details, may also be collected or displayed as part of gameplay where relevant.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: Information about your device, such as model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including feature usage and in-app behavior.
  • Log Data: Automatically collected data like IP addresses, timestamps, and crash reports.

1.3 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

We use Firebase, Google Analytics for Firebase, and Wiredash to help operate the App, understand how users interact with it, diagnose technical issues, and collect user feedback. These services may collect information such as device type, operating system, app version, app interaction events, crash reports, diagnostics, IP address, approximate location, and app instance or device identifiers.

If you submit feedback through the App, Wiredash may process the feedback content you provide and related diagnostic information. We do not intentionally send your uploaded photos or player names to analytics services unless needed to provide support or diagnose an issue you report.

1.4 Photo Library Access

The App may request access to your photo library so that you can select or provide photos for gameplay. Photo library access does not mean we copy your entire photo library. Photos are uploaded or processed only as needed to provide the gameplay features you use.

1.5 Safe Mode and Uncensored Mode

SnapShotz may offer different gameplay modes that affect how photos are selected and shown. In Safe Mode, players can review or allow photos before those photos are used in gameplay. In Uncensored Mode, photos may be selected from the photo library access you have granted without individual photo approval before they are shown, which means any eligible photo from that access may appear during the game. Uncensored Mode requires full or all-photos library access and may not work if you grant only limited photo access.

If you do not want certain photos to appear, use Safe Mode, limit the photo library access you grant to the App where your device allows it, or do not use Uncensored Mode.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the App and its features.
  • Facilitate gameplay, including photo sharing and leaderboard rankings.
  • Communicate with you about updates, promotions, or support.
  • Measure advertising performance and manage advertising or marketing where applicable.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use uploaded gameplay photos for advertising, analytics profiling, AI model training, or marketing unless you separately provide the content to us for support or give us permission.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Other Players in Your Game Room: Because photos are the core gameplay feature of SnapShotz, photos uploaded or selected for a game may be shown to other players in the same game room. Other players may be able to save, screenshot, screen record, photograph, or otherwise copy content shown during gameplay outside of our control.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors that help us operate, improve, and support the App, including Firebase, Google Analytics for Firebase, and Wiredash. These services may process device information, app usage events, diagnostics, crash logs, feedback submissions, and related technical data.
  • Advertising Providers: Third-party advertising providers may process identifiers, coarse location, advertising data, usage data, and device information to show ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and support advertising or marketing where applicable.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Photos and gameplay data are retained for the duration of the game session and deleted no later than 24 hours after the game ends. Analytics, diagnostics, crash, and feedback data may be retained by our third-party service providers according to their own retention practices and legal obligations.

Deleting photos and gameplay data from our systems does not remove copies that other players may have saved, screenshotted, screen recorded, photographed, or otherwise copied outside the App.

5. Security and Limitation of Liability

While we take reasonable steps to secure your personal data, we cannot guarantee complete security. In the event of a data breach or security incident, we shall not be held liable for any damages resulting from the breach. By using the App, you acknowledge this limitation of liability.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict data processing.
  • Data portability.

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@snapshotzdev.com.

7. International Users

If you use the App outside of North America, your data may be transferred to, stored, or processed in a country where privacy laws may not be as protective as those in your jurisdiction.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the “Last Updated” date.
