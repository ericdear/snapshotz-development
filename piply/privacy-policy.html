Piply Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Snapshotz Development, operating Piply (“we,” “us,” or “our”), respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how information is handled when you use the Piply mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

We do not require account creation and we do not collect personal information directly from you through our own database.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Advertising Data (via third-party ad providers): Device identifiers, approximate location, IP address, ad interactions, and diagnostic information may be collected by ad services to deliver and measure ads.
  • Device and Usage Data: Basic technical data (such as device type, operating system, language, and app interaction events) may be processed by third-party SDKs used in the App.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Show ads in the App.
  • Support app functionality and performance.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, or technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Advertising and Tracking Consent (Including Europe)

The App displays ads and may request your consent for ad tracking or personalized advertising where required by law.

  • EEA/UK/Switzerland users: You may be shown a consent form to choose whether to allow personalized ads.
  • Other regions: Consent prompts may still be shown where applicable platform rules or laws require it.

You can manage your ad/privacy choices in the app (when available), in your device settings, or through the options provided by the ad provider.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share information with:

  • Ad and analytics providers: To serve ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

We do not operate our own user database for Piply and we do not store user personal data on our own servers. Third-party providers may retain data according to their own policies and legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You may also have rights related to consent and ad personalization preferences.

To request help, contact us at support@snapshotzdev.com.

7. International Users

If you use the App outside of North America, information processed by third-party providers may be transferred to and processed in countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the “Last Updated” date.
