How Piply Works

A domino puzzle that stays readable.

Piply takes the familiar shape of dominoes and turns it into a focused logic puzzle: every move has to satisfy the board, the numbers, and the rules printed around each colored region.

1

Place Dominoes

Drag domino tiles onto adjacent cells and rotate them until each pip lands where it belongs.

2

Follow Region Rules

Colored areas ask for sums, zeroes, equal values, or other constraints that must all work together.

3

Solve With Logic

Use deduction, not guessing, to finish the board and sharpen your pattern recognition over time.
